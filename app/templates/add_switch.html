Need Help?
How to find your device's IP address:
  • Check your router: Log into your router's admin panel and look for connected devices
  • Use network scanner: Use tools like Fing (mobile app) or Advanced IP Scanner (desktop) to scan your network
  • Check device settings: Many smart devices show their IP address in the settings menu
Testing connectivity:

Before adding a switch, you can test if it's reachable by running:

ping 192.168.1.100
